Grammar: Habits

To talk about habits, we use the present and past simple as well as structures such as tend to and used to. We also use words and phrases such as rarely, not as much as and all the time to show how often.

  • My friends and I go out all the time.
  • I sometimes played it for eight or nine hours a day.
  • I don't tend to / I tend not to read much fiction.
  • Did you (ever) use to watch 'Betty la fea'?
  • We'd hang out all the time, but now we hardly ever do.
  • Not as a rule, no, but I do listen to some jazz every now and then.
